Academics

Our curriculum and academic programs

Curriculum Overview

Our curriculum is designed to provide a strong foundation in core subjects while encouraging critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills. We follow a comprehensive educational framework that prepares students for higher education and future careers.

Grade Levels

Kindergarten

Play-based learning focusing on motor skills, social development, and basic concepts

Primary (Classes 1-5)

Foundation in mathematics, science, languages, and social studies

Middle School (Classes 6-8)

Advanced concepts with introduction to specialized subjects

Secondary (Classes 9-10)

Board exam preparation with focus on core subjects

Higher Secondary (Classes 11-12)

Stream-specific education (Science, Commerce, Arts)

Teaching Methodology

We employ innovative teaching methods that include:

  • Interactive classroom sessions
  • Project-based learning
  • Group discussions and debates
  • Hands-on experiments in laboratories
  • Educational field trips
  • Multimedia-assisted learning

Assessment & Evaluation

Our assessment system is designed to provide continuous feedback and track student progress:

  • Regular unit tests and quizzes
  • Mid-term and final examinations
  • Project assessments
  • Practical examinations for science subjects
  • Parent-teacher meetings
  • Progress reports and report cards
