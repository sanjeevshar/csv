Admissions

Join our school community

Admission Process

  1. Registration: Fill out the registration form available at the school office or online
  2. Documentation: Submit required documents (birth certificate, previous school records, photographs)
  3. Assessment: Students may be required to take an entrance assessment
  4. Interview: Parent and student interview with the principal
  5. Admission Offer: Successful candidates will receive admission offers
  6. Fee Payment: Complete admission by paying the required fees

Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate (attested copy)
  • Transfer Certificate from previous school
  • Previous academic records/marksheets
  • Passport size photographs (4 copies)
  • Aadhar Card copy (student and parents)
  • Address proof
  • Medical certificate
